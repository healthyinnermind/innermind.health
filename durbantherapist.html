Ketamine Assisted Psychotherapy

Ketamine-assisted psychotherapy (KAP) is a treatment that uses prescribed doses of ketamine alongside psychotherapy. It has shown significant potential for conditions like treatment-resistant depression, anxiety, PTSD, OCD, and more. Ask me for more details.

Online Therapy

I offer online consultations for those who prefer or require remote therapy. Although in-person therapy is recommended, online therapy might suit those living far away or feeling anxious about starting therapy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Therapy and Counselling?

Therapy is simply talking to someone in a safe and confidential space to help you open up about feelings and challenges. Experienced therapists use their skills to help you make sense of what you're going through and gain clarity.

Does Therapy or Counselling Help?

These evidence-based interventions help with anxiety, depression, addiction, and emotional challenges. They help make positive changes in the way we think, feel, and behave, ultimately improving interpersonal relationships.

How Do I Know If I Need Counselling?

Everyone can benefit from counselling. If you find yourself stressed, anxious, overwhelmed, or experiencing negative patterns in your relationships or career, therapy can help.
